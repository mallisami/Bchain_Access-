\section*{S4. Security Threat Model (STRIDE)}
STRIDE decomposition of \texttt{HealthAccessControl.sol}. ``Residual/next step'' items are scoped to the
independent audit (Limitation~L8) and public-testnet evaluation (L7).
\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{|p{2.3cm}|p{5.4cm}|p{5.4cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{STRIDE category} & \textbf{Contract-level exposure} & \textbf{Mitigation / residual}\\\hline
\textbf{S}poofing (identity) & Impersonating a patient or provider address & EVM account keys + \texttt{onlyPatient(msg.sender)} binding; residual: key-management/HSM out of scope\\\hline
\textbf{T}ampering (data) & Altering records or grants & On-chain hash anchoring; append-only structures; off-chain records encrypted at rest (\conf{} KMS)\\\hline
\textbf{R}epudiation & Denying a grant/revoke occurred & Append-only \texttt{AuditEntry[]} audit log with immutable event trail\\\hline
\textbf{I}nformation disclosure & Leaking PII via chain data & Only 32-byte hashes on-chain; personal data off-chain; salted digests\\\hline
\textbf{D}enial of service & Blocking access or exhausting gas & \texttt{view} reads are free; bounded loops; residual: public-network gas/DoS under load unmeasured (L7)\\\hline
\textbf{E}levation of privilege & Gaining access without patient consent & Single-role \texttt{onlyPatient} modifier; two-phase time-locked confirmation; residual: static analysis (Slither/Mythril) recommended (see S6)\\\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\section*{S6. Static Analysis (Slither) --- Results}
A static analysis of \texttt{HealthAccessControl.sol} was performed using the Slither static analysis framework on 2026-07-08. The compiler used was Solidity v0.8.19 and Slither version was v0.11.5. No high- or medium-severity issues were found. The full analysis report is deposited in the public repository at \texttt{analysis/slither\_report.json}.
\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{|l|c|c|p{5.2cm}|}
\hline\textbf{Detector / severity} & \textbf{Count} & \textbf{Status} & \textbf{Resolution}\\\hline
High & 0 & Pass & No high-severity vulnerabilities found.\\\hline
Medium & 0 & Pass & No medium-severity vulnerabilities found.\\\hline
Low / Informational & 52 & Reviewed & Reviewed: 36 naming conventions, 10 block.timestamp comparisons for deliberate time-lock (acceptable), 2 string conversion helpers (false positives), 1 shadowing, 1 boolean comparison, 1 missing event, 1 solc version.\\\hline
Optimization & 0 & Pass & None.\\\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
